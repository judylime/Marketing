Tracking Progress

There are two widget projects to track in your meeting - Widget A and Widget B.

To add projects to your map:

  1. Select Project Status.
  2. Press the Insert key.
  3. Type "Widget A" in the subtopic and press Enter to finish editing.
  4. Press Enter again to create a sibling topic.
  5. Type "Widget B" in the subtopic and press Enter to finish editing.
