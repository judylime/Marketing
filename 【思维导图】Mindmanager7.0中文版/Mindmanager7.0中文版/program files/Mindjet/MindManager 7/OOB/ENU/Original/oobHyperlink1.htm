Hyperlinks

It's easy to link to outside data in MindManager.

You can link to web pages, other documents on your hard drive or network, even topics within another map.

Let's add a hyperlink to Widget B.

To add a hyperlink:

  1. Select Widget B.
  2. On the Topic menu, click Hyperlink .
  3. Type www.mindjet.com in the Link to field.
  4. Click OK. A hyperlink icon is added to the topic.
  5. To follow the link, just click on the icon.
